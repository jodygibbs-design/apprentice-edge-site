# Deloitte School Leaver Programme — ApprenticeEdge Prep Pack

## The Programme

**What it is:** Deloitte's BrightStart Higher Apprenticeship. You work full-time at Deloitte while studying for a professional qualification (AAT, ICAEW, ACCA, or CIMA depending on the stream). Not a degree — a professional qualification route. Faster to chartership than the degree route.

**Length:** 3–4 years to chartered status

**Salary:** £25,000–£30,000 starting (London weighting applied)

**Locations:** London, Birmingham, Bristol, Edinburgh, Leeds, Manchester, Reading, and others

**Streams available:**
- Audit & Assurance
- Tax
- Financial Advisory
- Consulting
- Risk Advisory
- Technology

**Qualification:** AAT → ACA/ACCA/CIMA depending on stream. All study costs paid by Deloitte.

**Application window:** Opens September/October for the following September start. BrightStart is highly competitive — Deloitte receives tens of thousands of applications per cycle.

## The Application Stages

### Stage 1 — Online Application
Personal details, academic grades, motivational questions (why Deloitte, why this service line).

**Key question:** "Tell us about a time you demonstrated one of Deloitte's values." Know their values before you apply: **lead the way, serve with integrity, take care of each other, foster inclusion, collaborate for measurable impact.**

### Stage 2 — Online Assessments
- **Immersive Online Assessment:** Deloitte uses a scenario-based immersive test (not traditional psychometric). You navigate a simulated work environment and make decisions. It takes about 45–60 minutes.
- **Numerical and verbal reasoning:** May be included depending on stream.

**Tip:** The immersive assessment tests judgement and values alignment, not just IQ. Read Deloitte's values page beforehand and let those inform your choices.

### Stage 3 — Video Interview
Pre-recorded via HireVue or similar. 3–5 questions, 30 seconds prep each.

**Typical questions:**
- Describe a time you worked under pressure to meet a deadline.
- Give an example of when you showed initiative.
- Why Deloitte — what specifically attracts you to BrightStart?
- Tell me about a time you had to adapt your communication style.
- Describe a situation where you had to analyse information to make a decision.

**Use STAR for every answer.** Keep Situation and Task brief — spend 60% of your time on Action and Result.

### Stage 4 — Assessment Centre
Half day at a Deloitte office. Includes:

1. **Group exercise** — business scenario, 20–30 mins. Assessors watch for collaboration and communication.
2. **Partner/Manager interview** — 45 min competency interview. Senior interviewer. Expect to be challenged on your answers ("What would you have done differently?").
3. **Written task** — analysis of a business case, produce a short recommendation.

**What scores highest at Deloitte AC:**
- Intellectual curiosity (asking good questions, not just answering them)
- Structured thinking (answer first, then explain — the consulting mindset)
- Humility with confidence (know your strengths, acknowledge gaps)

## Commercial Awareness — What You Must Know About Deloitte

**Revenue (FY2024):** $67bn globally. Deloitte is the largest of the Big Four by revenue.

**What Deloitte does:**
- **Audit & Assurance:** Independent verification of financial statements. Deloitte audits many of the FTSE 100.
- **Consulting:** Strategy, operations, technology transformation for large organisations.
- **Financial Advisory:** M&A, restructuring, forensic accounting, valuations.
- **Risk Advisory:** Regulatory compliance, cyber risk, internal audit.
- **Tax & Legal:** Corporate tax, transfer pricing, indirect taxes, legal services.
- **Technology:** Deloitte has a large tech delivery arm, including Deloitte Digital.

**Current Deloitte talking points (2025–26):**
- AI strategy and GenAI adoption in audit and consulting
- Deloitte's own AI tool "PairD" — rolled out to all staff
- Sustainability and climate reporting assurance
- Big Four regulatory scrutiny following audit scandals (KPMG, EY — Deloitte positioning as high-quality alternative)

**Strong answer structure:**

2. One thing about Deloitte's market position or culture
3. How it fits your specific career direction
