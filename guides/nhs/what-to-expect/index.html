What Makes NHS Different

The NHS prioritisation exercise is unique — you're given a large inbox of emails and tasks and must demonstrate how you'd manage competing demands and clinical priorities simultaneously. This is the stage where most candidates underperform.

The Honest Reality

The NHS Graduate Management Training Scheme (Degree Apprenticeship) is genuinely competitive — 4–7%acceptance rate. But the majority of rejected candidates don't fail because they weren't capable enough. They fail because they didn't prepare specifically enough for NHS's process.

The candidates who get offers are not necessarily the smartest in the room — they're the ones who treated the application as seriously as the job itself.
