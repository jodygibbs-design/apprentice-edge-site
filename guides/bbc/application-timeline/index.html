BBC Apprenticeship Application Timeline and Deadlines 2026

Salary: £20,000–£24,000Length: 18 months–2 yearsAcceptance: 3–5%

The BBC Apprenticeship application window is: Early in the year — roles typically open January–March. Here's how to plan your timeline so you're prepared at every stage — not scrambling.

When to Apply

Application window

Early in the year — roles typically open January–March

Apply in the first two weeks of the window opening. BBC makes rolling offers — seats fill before the official deadline closes.

Typical Timeline from Application to Offer

Week 0Application form submitted
Week 1–2Online assessments — SJT, numerical, and cognitive tests
Week 3–4Video interview invitation sent
Week 4–6Assessment centre invitation (if passed video stage)
Week 6–10Offer or rejection

Timeline is approximate. Actual timings vary by cohort and service line.

Preparation Timeline — Work Backwards

4 weeks before applyingResearch BBC — annual report, recent news, service lines
3 weeks before applyingDraft 5–7 STAR stories covering all key competencies
2 weeks before applyingPractice online tests with real providers (SHL, Korn Ferry)
1 week before applyingWrite and refine motivational answers — Why BBC?, Why now?
Before video interviewRecord and watch back a practice video interview
Before assessment centreRead BBC's latest news and know your STAR stories cold
