Commercial Awareness for EY Apprenticeship Applications

Salary: £22,000–£26,000Length: 5 yearsAcceptance: 3–5%

Commercial awareness is tested at every stage of the EYapplication — from motivational questions on the form through to case studies and interviews at the assessment centre. Here's what you need to know.

The Business Context

EY UK revenue exceeds £3.7 billion. Service lines include Assurance, Consulting, Strategy and Transactions, and Tax. EY recently dropped a planned global split (Project Everest), which is worth knowing about.

Key Facts to Know

Starting salary

£22,000–£26,000

Programme length

5 years

Acceptance rate

3–5%

Application window

October–January for the following September start

Commercial Awareness Questions to Prepare For

“What do you think are the biggest challenges facing EY in the next year?”

“Tell me something recent in the news that you think is relevant to what we do.”

“What trends are affecting our industry right now?”

“Can you explain what EY does in a couple of sentences?”

How to build commercial awareness fast

  • Read EY's latest annual report introduction (10 minutes — just the CEO letter)
  • Set a Google Alert for “EY” and read one article per week
  • Read the FT, BBC Business, or The Economist for 10 minutes each morning in the 4 weeks before your assessment
  • Be able to explain what EY does in two sentences without using industry jargon
