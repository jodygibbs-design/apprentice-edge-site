How to Get Into the Goldman Sachs Apprenticeship — Stage by Stage

Salary: £30,000–£35,000Length: 3 yearsAcceptance: 1–2%

The Goldman Sachs Higher Apprenticeship has an acceptance rate of around 1–2%. Here's exactly how to go from application to offer — stage by stage.

The Process, In Order

1

Online Application

Complete the application form — personal details, grades, and motivational questions. Application window: October–December for the following summer/autumn start.

2

Online Assessments

Numerical reasoning and coding/logic tests depending on division

3

Video Interview

HireVue pre-recorded — technical and motivational questions, 30 seconds prep per question

4

Assessment Centre

Multiple one-to-one interviews

What the Top Candidates Do Differently

  • ✓Apply in the first two weeks of the window opening — roles fill on a rolling basis
  • ✓Prepare 5–7 STAR stories before starting the application — you'll need them at multiple stages
  • ✓Know Goldman Sachs's business in detail — not just the sector. Goldman Sachs interviewers expect you to know the firm's recent deals and market position. Specific division knowledge is essential — a generic answer about 'wanting to work in finance' will not pass.
  • ✓Practice the online tests with the actual provider formats — not generic aptitude apps
  • ✓Record a video interview practice session and watch it back before the real thing
